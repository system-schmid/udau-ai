# UDAU Green Community Initiative Proposal

## Objective
Launch a community-led urban gardening project to promote sustainability, reduce carbon footprint, and foster local engagement through collaborative green spaces.

## Key Actions
1. **Site Acquisition**: Partner with municipal authorities to secure underutilized land plots.
2. **Volunteer Network**: Establish a 50+ member volunteer base for maintenance and education.
3. **Sponsorships**: Secure funding from eco-conscious local businesses for tools/equipment.
4. **Educational Workshops**: Monthly sessions on composting, native plant care, and water conservation.

## Expected Impact
- 20+ community gardens established within 12 months
- 500+ participants in sustainability programs
- 15% reduction in neighborhood waste through composting initiatives
