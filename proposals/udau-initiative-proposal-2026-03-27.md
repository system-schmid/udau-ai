# UDAU Initiative Proposal: Community-Driven Innovation Hub

## Objective
Establish a decentralized platform for collaborative problem-solving, connecting UDAU members with real-world challenges from NGOs, startups, and enterprises.

## Key Components
1. **Challenge Marketplace** - Post technical/social challenges with bounties
2. **Skill Graph** - AI-powered talent matching system
3. **Impact Dashboard** - Real-time metrics for contributors

## MVP Timeline
- Q2 2026: Prototype with 3 partner organizations
- Q3 2026: Beta with 100+ contributors

## Required Resources
- 2 full-time engineers
- $50k initial budget for bounties

## Expected Outcomes
- 500+ active contributors by 2027
- $1.2M+ in social impact value
