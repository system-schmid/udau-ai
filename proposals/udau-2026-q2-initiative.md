# UDAU 2026 Q2 Initiative Proposal

## Title
Autonomous Systems Ethics Framework Implementation

## Objective
Establish governance protocols for AI/robotic systems by June 2026, aligning with EU AI Act and IEEE P7000 standards.

## Key Components
- Risk assessment matrix for autonomous decision-making
- Human oversight escalation paths
- Bias mitigation in training data pipelines
- Audit trail requirements

## Timeline
- April: Stakeholder workshops
- May: Draft policy development
- June: Pilot implementation

## Resources Needed
- 2 ethics officers
- $150k budget allocation
- Cross-departmental working group

## Success Metrics
- 100% system compliance by Q3
- 50% reduction in ethical review backlogs
- Annual risk assessment completion rate >95%
