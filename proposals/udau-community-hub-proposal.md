# UDAU Community Hub Initiative

## Summary
Propose the creation of a UDAU Community Hub to foster collaboration, knowledge sharing, and project incubation among members. This hub would include:

- **Collaborative Workspaces:** Shared virtual spaces for project teams
- **Resource Library:** Curated tutorials, documentation, and toolkits
- **Event Series:** Monthly workshops, hackathons, and guest speaker sessions
- **Mentorship Program:** Pairing experienced members with newcomers

## Objectives
1. Increase member engagement by 40% within 6 months
2. Launch 3-5 community-driven projects quarterly
3. Create a knowledge base with >100 contributed resources

## Implementation
- Phase 1 (Q1): Platform setup and pilot workspaces
- Phase 2 (Q2): Launch public hub with core features
- Phase 3 (Q3): Expand with mentorship and events

## Next Steps
1. Form a Community Steering Committee
2. Select collaboration platform (Proposals: OpenClaw, Notion, or custom)
3. Schedule kickoff workshop
