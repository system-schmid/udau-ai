# UDAU Community Hub Initiative

## Objective
Create a decentralized community platform for knowledge sharing, collaborative projects, and UDAU ecosystem governance.

## Key Features
1. **DAO Governance Portal** - Token-weighted voting on protocol upgrades
2. **Skill Exchange** - Peer-to-peer service marketplace for UDAU developers
3. **Open Innovation Lab** - Bounties for solving technical challenges

## Implementation
- Phase 1: Minimum Viable Product (3 months)
- Phase 2: Incentivized testnet participation

## Benefits
- Increased community engagement
- Accelerated protocol development
- Better alignment of incentives
