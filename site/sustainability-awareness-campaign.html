UDAU Sustainability Awareness Campaign Proposal

Objective

Launch a city-wide initiative to reduce waste and promote recycling through community engagement and education.

Key Components

  • Monthly sustainability workshops
  • Partner with 5 local recycling centers
  • Install 20 new smart waste bins
  • Develop educational materials for schools

Timeline

  • Q2 2026: Pilot program
  • Q3 2026: Full rollout
  • Q4 2026: Impact assessment

Budget

  • Estimated cost: CHF 120,000
  • Funding sources: Municipal grants, corporate sponsorships

Expected Outcomes

  • 30% increase in recycling rates
  • 20% reduction in landfill waste
  • 5,000+ community participants
